The raw data from TCGA and METABRIC used in the study are available in the UCSC Cancer Genome Browser Xena (https://xenabrowser.net/) and the cBioportal website: (http://www.cbioportal.org), respectively. Approval by a local ethics committee was not required, and all the data can be immediately downloaded to serve for research purposes.
